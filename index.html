Simple Elegance

Whether you dream of a lavish banquet, glamorous wedding or intimate get-together, The Gathering Place invites you to take full advantage of 2,600 square feet of simple elegance. A dramatic 11-foot ceiling complete with 1940’s exposed beams enhances the space with a raw, industrial look not lacking in character or charm.

Hassle-Free

We happily provide our venue, including complete access to the high-functioning catering kitchen, limited tables and chairs, limited set-up and tear-down, and basic cleaning of the space after your event, all for one affordable price. A security deposit and event insurance are required to ensure your special event is stress-free.

Why The Gathering Place?

For the community, back to the community.
All of the profits from The Gathering Place go right back into our community through CLDI’s various areas of ministry outlined below.

  • Youth Works

    Youth Works’ mission is to ensure that every youth of the South Side has an opportunity to hear and to respond to the gospel of Jesus Christ and be discipled in His ways. We work primarily with youth from Riverside Middle School and Orchard Elementary.

  • Hannah House

    Our faith-based transitional home for women serves our community by providing a stable/sober living environment where the Gospel of Jesus can be heard and demonstrated. Our hope is to equip each woman we work with to have the relationships, skills, and mindset that will empower them to live purposeful, healthy lives.

  • CLDI Internship & Eleos Group

    The CLDI Internship exists to glorify God by recruiting and encouraging Kingdom-minded, eternally-focused disciples of Jesus Christ in the South Side neighborhood of Billings, Montana. Fred and Joan Nelson, Eleos Group, have been serving in the CLDI offices since 2001 offering their marriage, family care, discipleship instruction, and pastoral care.

  • Koinonia Management Company (KMC)

    We created KMC to specifically serve those who might have difficulty securing affordable housing, but not at the cost of quality. CLDI uses the landlord/tenant relationship to model and encourage honesty, accountability, responsibility, and love of neighbor.
